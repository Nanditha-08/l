Case Study

PropAgent AI Deep Dive

Automating a ₹50Cr Real Estate Business

UrbanNest Realty was drowning in leads but converting only 3%. We built a full AI-powered platform that transformed their entire operation in 45 days.

40%
Conversion Increase
200+
Daily Leads Processed
45
Days to Launch
🏡

⚠️ The Problem

UrbanNest had 200+ daily inbound leads from portals like 99acres and MagicBricks but only 6 sales agents. Leads were going cold within hours, follow-ups were inconsistent, and there was no unified tracking system. They were losing ₹2Cr/month in potential revenue.

💡 The Solution

We built a complete AI automation platform: instant WhatsApp responses, AI qualification chatbot, automatic lead scoring, CRM integration with Google Sheets, and a Telegram dashboard for the sales team to manage hot leads in real-time. AI calling agents handled initial outreach at scale.

⚙️ Technology Stack

React + Next.js frontend, Node.js backend, WhatsApp Business API, Twilio for calling, GPT-4 for natural language processing, PostgreSQL, and a custom webhook system connecting 8 lead sources into one unified pipeline.

📈 The Results

Within 90 days: lead response time dropped from 4 hours to 90 seconds, conversion rate climbed from 3% to 4.2%, agent productivity increased by 60%, and the business closed ₹8.5Cr in new sales attributable directly to the platform — a 17x ROI on their investment.
